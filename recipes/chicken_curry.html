Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Ground Masala: Fennel Seeds, Peppercorns, Cloves, Cardamom, Star Anise, Cinnamon
  • Garlic, Onion, Shallots and Ginger
  • Chilli
  • Curry Leaves

Steps

  1. Grind and get the masala by adding water.
  2. Cook the chicken for a bit and add the masala and water
